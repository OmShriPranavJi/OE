About the Author & Phewture

Krishnan Unni

Meet Krishnan Unni, the Founder of Pigtail Pundits (since 1997) and Chief Pundit at Phewture (since 2024).

With a distinguished career spanning over four decades, Unni has been at the forefront of transforming B2B businesses and their marketing teams.

His expertise lies in architecting impactful branding, content, and lead generation strategies that deliver tangible results.

As the Founder of Pigtail Pundits, his work, over 800 projects in 27+ years, is a testament to a deep-seated dedication to crafting compelling narratives - ones that connect businesses with their audiences, fostering significant growth and driving sales.

Today, Krishnan channels this wealth of experience and his passion for innovation into Phewture, where he serves as Chief Pundit.

Phewture is not just a platform. It's the culmination of 40 years of marketing wisdom and over 10,000 hours of deep immersion in generative AI.
