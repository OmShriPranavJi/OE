5.1: Brainstorming Marketing Ideas & Angles

Never Stare at a Blank Page Again

The Problem

The pressure to come up with fresh, relevant marketing ideas for your blog, social media, or promotional campaigns is constant. Creative blocks are common, and it's easy to fall into a rut or feel unsure about what your audience truly wants to see.

The AI Prompt Solution

Use AI as your creative brainstorming partner. By feeding it information about your business, target audience, and goals, you can generate a wealth of content ideas, different angles for topics, and even headline suggestions, breaking through those mental blocks and giving you a fantastic starting point.

Prompt Example 1: Generating Content Ideas for a Specific Audience

Why it Works

This prompt guides the AI to think specifically about your business and ideal customer. By asking for different types of content ideas (blogs, videos, campaigns), you get a diverse range of starting points to consider, saving you hours of solitary brainstorming.

Expected Outcome for You

A list of tailored content ideas and marketing angles that resonate with your target audience, helping you overcome creative blocks and fill your content calendar with relevant material much more easily.

Copy & Modify This Prompt

Act as a creative marketing strategist for a small business. I run a [Your Business Type, e.g., 'local bakery', 'freelance graphic design service', 'online eco-friendly store']. My target audience is primarily [Describe your ideal customer, e.g., 'busy parents looking for healthy treats', 'startups needing branding assistance', 'environmentally conscious shoppers aged 25-45'].

Please generate:

5-7 engaging blog post ideas or article topics relevant to this audience that also subtly promote my business.
3-4 ideas for a short video series or engaging social media content series.
2-3 potential themes for a promotional campaign or special offer.
Focus on ideas that address common pain points or interests of my target audience.

The Goal of this Refiner

To take the initial list of content ideas and explore different formats, consider search engine visibility, aim for specific audience reactions, or ensure a clear call to action is integrated.

Why This Refiner Works

This process helps transform broad ideas into more concrete and strategically aligned content plans, considering how the content will be found, consumed, and what action it should inspire.

Expected Enhanced Outcome for You

A set of content ideas that are not only creative and audience-relevant but also more versatile in format, potentially optimized for discoverability, and designed to achieve specific engagement goals or emotional responses.

Copy & Modify This Refiner Prompt

Review the list of generated content ideas for my [Your Business Type] [or paste the ideas here]. Now, refine these ideas further in ONE of the following ways:

1. Suggest Alternative Formats for an Idea: Take ONE of the blog post ideas and suggest 2-3 alternative ways the same core information could be presented (e.g., "as an infographic," "a short 'how-to' video script outline," "a downloadable checklist," "a series of interactive social media polls").

2. Identify Potential SEO Keywords: For TWO of the blog post or article ideas, suggest 3-4 relevant long-tail SEO keywords that a small business in my niche might realistically target to attract the specified audience.

3. Evoke a Specific Emotional Response: Choose ONE of the promotional campaign themes. Suggest 2-3 specific angles or messaging approaches that would aim to evoke a particular emotion in the target audience (e.g., for a theme of 'Spring Refresh,' angles to evoke 'excitement,' 'relief,' or 'inspiration').

4. Integrate Clear Calls to Action: For three of the generated content ideas (blog or social media), suggest a specific and relevant call to action (CTA) that should accompany each piece of content.
