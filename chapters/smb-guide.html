How to Use This eBook

This eBook contains numerous AI prompts designed to help solve specific business challenges. Here's how to make the most of them:

  1. Choose Your LLM: The prompts in this eBook work with virtually any Large Language Model, including:
    • Google's Gemini Pro 2.5
    • Perplexity.ai
    • ChatGPT by OpenAI
    • DeepSeek
    • Kimi.ai
    • Meta's Llama 3
    • And many others
  2. Expect Variation: Different LLMs will produce slightly different results when using these prompts. This is normal and expected - each has its own strengths and approaches.
  3. Customize as Needed: Feel free to modify the prompts to better suit your specific business needs or to optimize for your preferred LLM.
  4. Iterate for Best Results: If the first response doesn't fully address your needs, try using the Refiner prompts we've included with each main prompt in the eBook. These Refiner prompts are specifically designed to help you get more detailed or focused results.

While the exact wording or format of responses may vary between models, all major LLMs should be able to deliver the insights and strategies you're looking for.
